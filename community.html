2025 / Adakluto The climb that made the crew real.
Hike Adakluto 2025 16 people

The first climb everyone remembered.

16 people. Early call time. Long bus ride. Most had never met. Then the climb started. Adakluto gave the group a real test: pace, patience, and the reward at the top. By the time they came down, the crew already felt different.

The trip that proved the route mattered as much as the people.

2025 / Asenema No flyer. Just a text.
Asenema Waterfalls 2025 14 people

The one with no flyer.

No flyer. No Instagram post. Zico just texted his people. The spots filled in two days. This was the moment Adventures of Life became something real. The community was already there — it just needed to be activated.

The trip that proved the community existed.

2026 / Ada The quieter route with the strongest frames.
Ada / Nkyinkyim Museum Museum day

The route that slowed everything down.

Ada changed the rhythm of the archive. Less climb, more sequence. Sculpture grounds first, then the movement out toward the water, then the quieter portrait and lagoon frames that made the whole day feel longer than it was.

The trip that proved the catalogue can hold culture, water, and softer energy too.

2025 / Akwamu The hike that conquered them.
Akwamu Gorge 2025 20 people

The hike nobody expected.

20 people at Madina Station. The gorge hike was brutal. But they got to the top. The view was mind-blowing. The canopy walk after made it perfect. The photos went everywhere. New people started asking to join.

The trip that made Adventures of Life visible.

Dec 2025 / Keta 33 people. Three days. Best one yet.
Keta 3-Day Trip / December 2025 33 people

The biggest one.

Christmas season. Keta. Three days — the Volta experience, the mountain, the beach camp, a house party until sunrise. 33 people came as strangers. They left as something else entirely. This trip defined what Adventures of Life is.

The fastest sell-out. The most energy. Still talked about.
