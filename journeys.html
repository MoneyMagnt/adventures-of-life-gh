FAQs

Answer the questions global travelers are most likely to ask.

Clear answers increase trust and help the brand feel prepared for international guests.

Do you help with airport pickup?

Yes. Airport welcome support is one of the easiest ways to reduce anxiety for first-time arrivals.

Can groups request private trips?

Yes. The site is positioned to attract private groups, retreats, and creator collectives alongside public departures.

Can trips include more than one country?

Yes. Ghana-led itineraries can extend into Togo, Benin, and custom neighboring-country options.

Do you support diaspora travelers?

Yes. The messaging, pacing, and route style are intentionally built to feel welcoming and grounded.
